Recommendations - from the Founding Team

 I’m excited to break the ice and bring you the first recommendation! The book I’ve been absolutely hooked on this past month is ‘The Song of the Cell’ by Pulitzer Prize-winning author Siddhartha Mukherjee. It’s been my go-to companion on these cold, rainy November nights—perfect for curling up in bed after a long day.

 This captivating read takes you on a journey through groundbreaking discoveries in the molecular world. Each chapter dives into a new scientific milestone, blending the history of these achievements with Mukherjee’s heartfelt anecdotes from his life as a doctor and researcher. What makes it truly special is how the author breaks down complex biological concepts into engaging, story-like narratives that anyone can enjoy.

 I particularly loved the first part of the book, which beautifully traces the history of the cell's discovery. For the first time, I felt like I had a complete picture of the timeline—it’s both enlightening and inspiring.

 This book is also perfect for the busy school season. With each chapter taking less than an hour to read, it’s easy to squeeze into a packed schedule, leaving you feeling enriched. If you’re looking for a book that’s as educational as it is enjoyable, this is it!

 Carolina
